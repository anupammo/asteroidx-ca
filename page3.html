How Does Amazon DSP Work?

Amazon Demand-Side-Platform is a comparatively new advertising format and also belongs to Amazon advertising. Display campaigns can be run via publishers (apps or third-party websites) via DSP. Specific target groups can be controlled based on Amazon first-party data. Targeting options include demographic, lifestyle, in-market, audience look-alikes, custom, contextual details, and retargeting.
